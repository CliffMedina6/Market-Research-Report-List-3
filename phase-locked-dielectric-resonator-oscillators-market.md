<p><p>Phase-locked Dielectric Resonator Oscillators are divided into two main types: Single Loop and Double Loop. Single Loop oscillators rely on a single feedback loop to stabilize the output frequency, making them simpler and less expensive. In contrast, Double Loop oscillators use two feedback loops, allowing for even better frequency stability and phase noise performance. While Single Loop oscillators are more commonly used in simpler applications, Double Loop oscillators are preferred for high-performance and precision applications.</p></p>
