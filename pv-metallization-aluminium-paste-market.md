<p><ul><li>BSF Solar Cells</li><li>PERC Solar Cells</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PV Metallization Aluminium Paste is used in the production of various types of solar cells, including BSF and PERC cells. BSF (Back Surface Field) solar cells have a simple structure, with metallization paste applied to the back surface to help improve efficiency. On the other hand, PERC (Passivated Emitter and Rear Cell) cells feature a more advanced design, where the rear surface is passivated to minimize recombination of charge carriers. The metallization paste used in each type of cell is tailored to their specific requirements for optimal performance.</p></p>
