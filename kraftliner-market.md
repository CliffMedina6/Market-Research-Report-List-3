<p><p>Kraftliner is a type of paperboard used in packaging and is available in two main market types: Bleached (White-top) Kraftliner and Unbleached (Brown) Kraftliner. Bleached Kraftliner has a white top layer, giving it a clean and professional appearance. It is often used for high-end packaging applications where visual appeal is important. Both types of Kraftliner offer strength, durability, and sustainability for various packaging needs.</p></p>
