Fluorescence Microscopy utilizes fluorescent dyes or proteins to visualize apoptotic cells under a microscope. The "Others" category encompasses alternative methods such as enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay (ELISA) and immunohistochemistry (IHC) that are used to detect and quantify apoptosis. These different types offer researchers and clinicians diverse options for studying and analyzing apoptosis.</p></p>
