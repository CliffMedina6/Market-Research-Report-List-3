<p><ul><li>Single</li><li>Dual</li><li>Multi-head</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Motorized decoiler machines are available in various types based on the number of mandrels they have. Single-head decoilers have one mandrel, dual-head decoilers have two mandrels, and multi-head decoilers have three or more mandrels. Single-head decoilers are ideal for smaller operations with less variety in coil sizes. Dual-head decoilers provide more flexibility in handling different coil sizes and materials. Multi-head decoilers are suitable for high-volume production environments where quick changeovers between different coil sizes are necessary. Each type offers different capacities and features to meet specific needs in the market.</p></p>
