<p><p>Tire protection chains come in three main types: Link-Ring-System, Ring-Ring-System, and Ring-Locking-System. The Link-Ring-System consists of links connected by rings, providing flexibility and improved traction. The Ring-Ring-System features rings connected to each other, offering enhanced durability and strength. The Ring-Locking-System involves rings that lock together, providing maximum security and stability. Each type caters to different needs and preferences, offering various benefits for users depending on their specific requirements.</p></p>
