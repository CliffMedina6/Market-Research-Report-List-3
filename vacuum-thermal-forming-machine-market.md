<p><p>Semi-Automatic Thermal Forming Machines require some level of manual intervention during the forming process, such as loading materials or removing finished products. Fully Automatic Thermal Forming Machines, on the other hand, operate without the need for manual assistance, from material loading to product ejection. While semi-automatic machines may offer more flexibility and lower initial costs, fully automatic machines are preferred for high-volume production due to their efficiency and reduced labor requirements. Both types cater to different market needs and production scales.</p></p>
