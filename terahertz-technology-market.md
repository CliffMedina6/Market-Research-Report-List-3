<p><p>The Terahertz Technology Market includes two main types of products: Terahertz sources and Terahertz detectors. Terahertz sources are devices that generate Terahertz waves, which are electromagnetic waves with frequencies between microwave and infrared. These sources are used in numerous applications such as spectroscopy, imaging, and communication. On the other hand, Terahertz detectors are devices that capture and measure Terahertz waves. They are crucial for detecting and analyzing Terahertz radiation in various fields, including medical diagnostics, security screening, and material characterization. Both Terahertz sources and detectors form integral segments of the Terahertz Technology Market.</p></p>
