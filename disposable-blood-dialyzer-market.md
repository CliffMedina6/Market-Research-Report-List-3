<p><strong>The Disposable Blood Dialyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-Flux Dialyzer</li><li>Low-Flux Dialyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable blood dialyzer market consists of two main types: high-flux dialyzers and low-flux dialyzers. High-flux dialyzers have larger pore sizes which enable efficient clearance of waste products from blood during dialysis. They are suitable for patients with high levels of toxins. On the other hand, low-flux dialyzers have smaller pore sizes and are mainly used for patients with lower levels of toxins. Both types of dialyzers are designed to be used once and discarded, ensuring safety and hygiene during dialysis treatments.</p></p>
