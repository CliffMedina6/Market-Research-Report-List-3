<p><p>A vacuum mattress stretcher is a medical device used for immobilization and transportation of patients with suspected spinal injuries. It is made of a durable, airtight PVC material and is designed to mold and conform to the patient's body shape when it is evacuated of air. This provides a safe and secure means of immobilizing the patient during transport, reducing the risk of further injury.</p><p>The vacuum mattress stretcher market is expected to experience strong growth in the coming years. Factors such as an increase in the number of road accidents, sports injuries, and spinal injuries, along with advancements in healthcare infrastructure, are driving the demand for vacuum mattress stretchers. Additionally, the rising geriatric population and the resulting increase in age-related injuries are contributing to the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing various industry trends that are shaping its growth. One such trend is the development of lightweight and portable vacuum mattress stretchers, which are more convenient for emergency medical services to carry and use. Another trend is the incorporation of advanced features such as X-ray translucent properties, which enable medical professionals to perform imaging tests without removing the patient from the stretcher.</p><p>Overall, the vacuum mattress stretcher market is expected to grow at a significant rate during the forecast period, with a projected compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 14%.
