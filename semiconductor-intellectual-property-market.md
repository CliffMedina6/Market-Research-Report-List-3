<p><strong>The Semiconductor Intellectual Property Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hard IP Cores</li><li>Soft IP Cores</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor intellectual property (IP) market consists of two main types: hard IP cores and soft IP cores. Hard IP cores are pre-designed and physically implemented semiconductor blocks that are ready for integration into a chip design. They provide specific functionality such as memory or processors. On the other hand, soft IP cores are more flexible and customizable. They are essentially design descriptions that require further synthesis and customization to be implemented. Both types of IP cores are licensable and play a crucial role in accelerating chip development and reducing design costs.</p></p>
