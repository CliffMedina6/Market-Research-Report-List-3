<p><strong>The Nuclear Primary Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactor Coolant Pump</li><li>Condensate Pump</li><li>Feed Water Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nuclear Primary Pump market consists of three main types: Reactor Coolant Pump, Condensate Pump, and Feed Water Pump. The Reactor Coolant Pump circulates coolant within the nuclear reactor to remove heat generated during the nuclear fission process. The Condensate Pump removes condensed steam from the condenser to maintain proper pressure levels in the steam cycle. The Feed Water Pump is responsible for supplying water to the steam generator to create steam for the turbine. Each type plays a crucial role in the operation of nuclear power plants.</p></p>
