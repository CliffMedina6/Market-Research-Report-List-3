<p><p>Pig farming market types can be categorized into different systems based on the stages of production. Farrow-to-finish farms manage the entire lifecycle of pigs from birth to market. Farrow-to-nursery farms focus on raising piglets until they are ready for the nursery phase. Farrow-to-wean farms specialize in breeding sows and raising piglets until weaning. Wean-to-finish farms take over from weaning until market weight is reached. Finishing farms solely focus on raising pigs to market weight. Each market type caters to different stages of pig production.</p></p>
