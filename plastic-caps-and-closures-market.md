<p><p>Plastic caps and closures market can be broadly classified into three types: PP caps and closures, PE caps and closures, and handheld plastic caps and closures. PP caps and closures are made from polypropylene, which offers excellent chemical resistance. PE caps and closures, on the other hand, are made from polyethylene, which provides durability and flexibility. Handheld plastic caps and closures are designed for ease of use and are commonly used in packaging for beverages, personal care products, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
