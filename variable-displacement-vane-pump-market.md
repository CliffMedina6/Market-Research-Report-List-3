<p><p>Variable Displacement Vane Pump Market Types are categorized into Direct Operated and Pilot Operated. Direct Operated pumps are controlled by mechanical means to vary the displacement, while Pilot Operated pumps use hydraulic pressure to adjust the displacement. Direct Operated pumps are simple and cost-effective, suitable for applications with steady loads. On the other hand, Pilot Operated pumps offer precise control and are commonly used in applications where variations in pressure and flow are required. Both types cater to different market needs based on their characteristics and functionalities.</p></p>
