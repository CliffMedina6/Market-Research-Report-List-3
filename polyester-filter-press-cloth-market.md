<p><p>Polyester Filter Press Cloth is classified into two main types: Monofilament Filter Press Cloth and Multifilament Filter Press Cloth. Monofilament Filter Press Cloth is made up of single yarns with larger diameters, providing high strength and quick cake release. On the other hand, Multifilament Filter Press Cloth is composed of multiple smaller yarns twisted together, offering better particle retention and filtration efficiency. Both types are widely used in industrial applications for solid-liquid separation processes.</p></p>
