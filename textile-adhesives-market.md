<p><p>Textile adhesives are classified into three main types based on the materials they are designed to bond: Polyolefin (PO), Polyamide (PA), and Polyester (PES). Polyolefin adhesives are specifically formulated to bond materials like polyethylene and polypropylene. Polyamide adhesives are commonly used for nylon fabrics and other synthetic materials. Polyester adhesives are ideal for bonding polyester fabrics and other polyester-based materials. Each type of adhesive offers unique properties and capabilities tailored to accommodate specific textile bonding needs.</p></p>
