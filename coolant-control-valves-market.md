<p><p>Coolant control valves are devices used in automotive and industrial applications to regulate the flow of coolant within a system. There are two main types of coolant control valves: 2-way and 3-way. </p><p>2-way valves have two ports - an inlet and an outlet - and are commonly used to turn the coolant flow on or off.</p><p>3-way valves have three ports and are used to divert coolant flow between two different paths. They can control multiple functions such as regulating temperature or directing coolant to specific components in a system.</p></p>
