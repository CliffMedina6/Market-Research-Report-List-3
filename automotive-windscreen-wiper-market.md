<p><p>The automotive windscreen wiper market consists of two main types: wiper blades and wiper arms. Wiper blades are the rubber or silicone strips that come into direct contact with the windscreen to wipe away water, dirt, and debris. Wiper arms are the metal components that hold the wiper blades in place and move them across the windscreen. Both wiper blades and wiper arms are essential components of a vehicle's wiper system, ensuring clear visibility for the driver in various weather conditions.</p></p>
