<p><p>A drainage stent, also known as a biliary stent or a ureteral stent, is a medical device that is inserted into the body to maintain the patency of the duct or vessel for proper drainage. It is commonly used in various medical procedures such as managing obstruction in the bile or urinary tract due to tumors, strictures, or stones. It helps in relieving pain, preventing infections, and reducing the risk of complications.</p><p>The drainage stent market has been witnessing significant growth due to the rising prevalence of diseases such as kidney stones, chronic pancreatitis, and biliary diseases. Additionally, advancements in stent technology, such as the development of self-expanding metal stents and drug-eluting stents, have improved patient outcomes and contributed to market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing geriatric population, who are more susceptible to various diseases requiring drainage stents, is also driving market growth. The rise in minimally invasive procedures and the need for less invasive treatment options are further propelling the demand for drainage stents.</p><p>The market for drainage stents is segmented based on type, material, application, end-user, and region.
