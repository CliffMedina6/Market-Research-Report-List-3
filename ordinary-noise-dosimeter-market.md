<p><p>Ordinary noise dosimeters are available in two main types: with cable and cableless. The dosimeters with cable are connected to a microphone via a cable, allowing for easy placement on a worker's clothing. On the other hand, cableless dosimeters are designed with built-in microphones and data storage capabilities, eliminating the need for cables. Both types offer accurate noise level measurements for monitoring occupational noise exposure, with the choice between them depending on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
