<p><p>Polyimide (PI) is a versatile polymer used in various industries due to its excellent thermal stability, mechanical properties, and chemical resistance. There are three main types of PI based on their chemical structure: aliphatic (linear polyimides), semi-aromatic, and aromatic. Aliphatic PIs have flexible chains, making them more soluble and processable. Semi-aromatic PIs have a balance of properties between aliphatic and aromatic PIs. Aromatic PIs have rigid chains, providing superior thermal and chemical resistance but lower solubility and processability. Each type of PI serves different applications based on their unique properties.</p></p>
