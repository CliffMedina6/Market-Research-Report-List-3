<p><strong>The Modified Bitumen Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SBS Type</li><li>APP Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Modified Bitumen Membrane Market consists of two main types - SBS Type and APP Type. SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene) Type membranes are composed of a blend of asphalt and elastomers that provide flexibility, durability, and resistance to temperature fluctuations. APP (Atactic Polypropylene) Type membranes are made by blending asphalt with plasticizers to enhance UV resistance and improve overall performance. Both types offer excellent waterproofing properties, ease of installation, and long-term protection for roofs in commercial and residential applications.</p></p>
