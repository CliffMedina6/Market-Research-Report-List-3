<p><p>Polyelectrolytes are classified into three main types based on their charge: Polysalts, Polycations, and Polyanions. Polysalts are composed of both positively and negatively charged groups, making them neutral overall. Polycations carry a positive charge, while polyanions carry a negative charge. Each type of polyelectrolyte has unique properties and applications based on their charge characteristics. These diverse types cater to a wide range of industries such as water treatment, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and many others.</p></p>
