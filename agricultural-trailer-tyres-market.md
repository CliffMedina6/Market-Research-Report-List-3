<p><p>The agricultural trailer tyres market consists of two main types: Radial Agriculture Tires and Bias (Crossply) Agriculture Tires. Radial agriculture tires have cords that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, providing better traction, fuel efficiency, and a smoother ride. Bias or crossply agriculture tires have cords that crisscross at an angle, offering greater stability and durability on rough terrain. Both types cater to different needs of farmers depending on the conditions in which they operate their equipment.</p></p>
