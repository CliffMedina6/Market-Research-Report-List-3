<p><p>Knife mills are available in two main types - pulley knife mills and without pulley knife mills. Pulley knife mills are equipped with a pulley system that allows for easy manual operation, making them suitable for small-scale grinding tasks. On the other hand, without pulley knife mills operate using electric power, making them more efficient for larger scale operations. Both types of knife mills are commonly used in industries such as food processing, agriculture, and pharmaceuticals for grinding and reducing the size of various materials.</p></p>
