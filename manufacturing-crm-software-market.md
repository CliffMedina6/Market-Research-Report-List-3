<p><p>Manufacturing CRM software is available in two types: cloud-based and on-premises market. Cloud-based CRM software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, allowing manufacturers to access their CRM system from any location with an internet connection. On the other hand, on-premises CRM software is installed and managed on the manufacturer's own local servers and infrastructure. This allows manufacturers to have more control over their data and customization options, but requires additional hardware and IT support.</p></p>
