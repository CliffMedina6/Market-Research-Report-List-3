<p><p>Copper CMP slurries are categorized into two market types: Prestonian Type and Non-Prestonian Type. Prestonian Type slurries have a high concentration of abrasive particles, making them suitable for aggressive polishing processes. In contrast, Non-Prestonian Type slurries contain a lower concentration of abrasive particles, making them more suitable for less aggressive polishing applications. Both types offer specific benefits and are tailored to meet the varying needs of the copper CMP industry.</p></p>
