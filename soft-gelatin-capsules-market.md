<p><p>Soft gelatin capsules are pharmaceutical dosage forms that are used to encapsulate liquid or semi-solid active ingredients. They are classified into two main types based on the gelatin used: gelatin type and non-animal type. Gelatin type capsules are made from animal-derived gelatin, which is obtained from collagen. On the other hand, non-animal type capsules are made from alternative materials such as cellulose or starch. Both types offer advantages and disadvantages depending on factors like stability requirements, religious or cultural considerations, and specific patient needs.</p></p>
