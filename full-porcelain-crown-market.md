<p><p>The Full Porcelain Crown market offers various types of materials for dental crowns. Silica, Alumina, and Zirconia are commonly used ceramics. Silica crowns are aesthetically pleasing but can be brittle. Alumina provides high strength but lacks translucency. Zirconia crowns offer both strength and aesthetic appeal. Monolithic Zirconia crowns are made through a milling process and are highly durable. Lithium-Disilicate crowns provide excellent aesthetics and strength. These different materials cater to different patient needs and preferences, ensuring a range of options for dental professionals to choose from.</p></p>
