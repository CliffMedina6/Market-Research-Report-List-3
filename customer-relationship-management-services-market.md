Cloud-based CRM is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. On-premise CRM, on the other hand, is installed on local servers and managed by the company's IT department, providing more control over data security but requiring higher maintenance and infrastructure costs.
