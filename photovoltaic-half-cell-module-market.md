<p><p>Photovoltaic half-cell module market includes two main types: Monocrystalline half-cell and Polycrystalline half-cell. Monocrystalline half-cell modules are made from high-purity silicon, offering higher efficiency due to their uniform structure and higher conversion rate of sunlight into electricity. Polycrystalline half-cell modules, on the other hand, are made from multiple silicon crystals, providing a more cost-effective option with slightly lower efficiency. Both types offer advantages in terms of durability, performance, and cost-efficiency for solar energy systems.</p></p>
