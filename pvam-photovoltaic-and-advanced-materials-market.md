<p><p>PVAM (Photovoltaic And Advanced) Materials Market includes three main types: Metallization Pastes, Films, and Solar Silicones. Metallization pastes are used for the front and rear contacts of solar cells, improving their efficiency. Films are thin layers applied to solar panels to enhance light absorption and increase energy conversion. Solar silicones are used for encapsulation and sealing purposes, protecting the solar cells from environmental factors. These materials play a crucial role in the production and performance of photovoltaic systems.</p></p>
