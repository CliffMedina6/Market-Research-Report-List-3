<p><ul><li>Ferrite Core</li><li>Powder Core</li><li>Amorphous Core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Soft magnetic cores are materials used in electrical devices to enhance and focus magnetic fields. The three main types of soft magnetic cores are ferrite cores, powder cores, and amorphous cores. Ferrite cores are made of iron oxide and have high resistance to magnetic flux, making them ideal for high-frequency applications. Powder cores are made of iron powder mixed with insulating material, providing high power density and low core loss. Amorphous cores are made of non-crystalline metal alloys and offer low core loss and high permeability.</p></p>
