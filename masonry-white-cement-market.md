<p><p>Masonry White Cement Market offers different types including Type N and Type S. Type N cement is commonly used for general construction applications where moderate strength is required. It is suitable for above-grade walls, retaining walls, and paving stones. On the other hand, Type S cement is designed for use in structural applications where high strength is needed. It is ideal for foundations, walls, and other load-bearing structures. Both types of cement provide excellent durability and workability for various masonry projects.</p></p>
