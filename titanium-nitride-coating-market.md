<p><p>Titanium nitride coating market can be categorized into two main types: Chemical Vapor Deposition and Physical Vapor Deposition. Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) involves the use of chemical reactions to deposit a thin layer of titanium nitride onto a substrate, while Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) involves the use of physical processes like sputtering or evaporation to create the coating. Both methods are widely used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and medical for their excellent wear resistance and decorative properties.</p></p>
