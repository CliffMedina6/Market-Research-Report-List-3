<p><p>Neurovascular embolization devices are used to treat various conditions such as aneurysms and blood vessel abnormalities in the brain. The market for these devices includes embolization coils, flow diversion stents, liquid embolic systems, and aneurysm clips. Embolization coils are used to block blood flow to an abnormal blood vessel, while flow diversion stents divert blood flow away from the aneurysm. Liquid embolic systems involve injecting a substance to block blood flow, and aneurysm clips are used to close off the bulging blood vessel.</p></p>
