<p><p>The utility audit software market comprises two main types: cloud-based and installed-PC solutions. Cloud-based software operates on remote servers, allowing users to access and manage data through internet connections without the need for installing software on individual devices. This type offers flexibility, scalability, and ease of use. On the other hand, installed-PC software is installed directly on personal computers and operates offline. It provides users with more control over their data and may be preferred for security reasons or when internet connectivity is limited.</p></p>
