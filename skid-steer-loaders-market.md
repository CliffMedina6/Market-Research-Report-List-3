<p><p>The skid-steer loaders market can be categorized into two types: wheeled skid-steer loaders and tracked skid-steer loaders. Wheeled skid-steer loaders are equipped with four wheels, providing excellent maneuverability and speed on even surfaces. They are suitable for applications that require high versatility and quick transportation between job sites. On the other hand, tracked skid-steer loaders use tracks instead of wheels, offering enhanced stability and traction on uneven terrains, such as construction sites. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the project and the surface conditions where the equipment will be operated.</p></p>
