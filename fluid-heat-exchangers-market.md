<p><ul><li>Plate-fin Type</li><li>Wall Type</li><li>Hybrid Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluid heat exchangers are devices that transfer thermal energy between fluids at different temperatures. There are three main types in the market. Plate-fin type heat exchangers feature stacked plates with fluid passages for efficient heat transfer. Wall type heat exchangers consist of a series of tubes enclosed within a shell to separate the two fluids. Hybrid type heat exchangers combine the characteristics of both plate-fin and wall type, allowing for versatile applications. These types effectively facilitate heat exchange between fluids, enabling various industrial processes.</p></p>
