<p><p>Cast film is a type of plastic film that is produced by the process of extrusion where the molten plastic is forced through a flat die to create a thin sheet. This sheet is then cooled rapidly to solidify before being wound into rolls. Cast film is known for its excellent clarity, smooth surface finish, and high tensile strength, making it suitable for various applications such as food packaging, industrial packaging, agricultural films, and construction materials.</p><p>The cast film market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.1% during the forecast period.
