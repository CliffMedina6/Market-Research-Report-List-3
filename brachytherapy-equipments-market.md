<p><p>Brachytherapy Equipments Market includes two main types: High Dose Rate (HDR) and Low Dose Rate (LDR). HDR brachytherapy involves delivering radiation at a high dose rate over a short period of time, typically using a remote afterloader device. On the other hand, LDR brachytherapy involves delivering radiation at a low dose rate continuously over a longer period of time, often using radioactive seeds or implants. Both types of brachytherapy have their unique applications and advantages in the treatment of various cancers.</p></p>
