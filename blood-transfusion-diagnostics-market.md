The market is primarily driven by the rising demand for safe and effective blood transfusions, increasing prevalence of chronic diseases requiring blood transfusions, and advancements in technology in blood screening and typing. <p><p>The Blood Transfusion Diagnostics market includes various types of diagnostic technologies such as ELISA, NAT (Nucleic Acid Testing), Western Blot Assays, Fluorescence Assay, and other technologies. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) is commonly used to detect and quantify antibodies or antigens. NAT involves the detection of viral genetic material in blood. Western Blot Assays detect specific proteins within a sample. Fluorescence Assay utilizes fluorescent molecules to detect specific antibodies or antigens. Other technologies may include molecular diagnostics, rapid diagnostic tests, or immunohematology techniques. These technologies play a crucial role in ensuring safe and accurate blood transfusions.</p></p>
