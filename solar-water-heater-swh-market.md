<p><p>Solar Water Heater (SWH) market is segmented into two main types: Compact Type and Split Type. Compact Type consists of a single unit where the solar collector and storage tank are integrated, making it easy to install and maintain. On the other hand, Split Type has a separated system where the collector is installed on the roof and the storage tank is placed indoor, providing more flexibility in installation and design. Both types offer energy-efficient solutions for heating water using solar power.</p></p>
