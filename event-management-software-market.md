<p><p>Event management software is available in two major types: On-Premises and Cloud-Based. On-Premises software is installed and run on a company's own servers or computers, requiring regular maintenance and updates. It offers greater control and customization but comes with higher upfront costs and limited accessibility. On the other hand, Cloud-Based software is hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed via the internet. It offers scalability, flexibility, and accessibility from anywhere, with lower upfront costs and automatic updates. Users can choose the type that best suits their needs and budget.</p></p>
