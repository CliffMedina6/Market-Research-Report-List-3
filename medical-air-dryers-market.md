<p><p>Medical air dryers are devices used to remove moisture from compressed air, ensuring it is clean and dry for medical applications. There are two main types of medical air dryers: single tube dryers and multi-tube dryers. Single tube dryers use a single tube to circulate compressed air, while multi-tube dryers utilize multiple tubes. Both types function by passing compressed air through a desiccant material, which absorbs the moisture, resulting in dry and sterile air for medical equipment and procedures.</p></p>
