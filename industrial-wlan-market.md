Wireless LAN Controllers manage and control multiple access points. Multigigabit Switching provides high-speed connectivity for the WLAN infrastructure. Wireless Location Appliance assists in tracking and locating devices within the network. These components collectively contribute to a reliable and efficient WLAN network in industrial environments.</p></p>
