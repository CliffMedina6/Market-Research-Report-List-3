<p><p>Deep Packet Inspection (DPI) technology is used to inspect and manage network traffic for security, compliance, and performance optimization. The DPI market is divided into two main types: Standalone DPI and Integrated DPI. Standalone DPI solutions are deployed as separate devices dedicated solely to DPI functions. On the other hand, Integrated DPI involves incorporating DPI capabilities into existing network devices such as firewalls or routers. Both types serve the purpose of analyzing and controlling data packets traversing the network.</p></p>
