<p><strong>The Compact Industrial Metal AM Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder Bed Fusion (PBF)</li><li>Material Extrusion (ME)</li><li>Directed Energy Deposition (DED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Compact Industrial Metal AM Printer market consists of three main types: Powder Bed Fusion (PBF), Material Extrusion (ME), and Directed Energy Deposition (DED). PBF involves selectively melting metal powder layers using a laser or electron beam. ME, also known as metal 3D printing, extrudes metal materials layer by layer through a nozzle. DED utilizes a heat source to melt and fuse metal powders onto a substrate. These technologies are used in compact printers to create industrial metal parts with high precision and efficiency.</p></p>
