<p><strong>The All-Wheel Drive Motorcycles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Disc Braking System</li><li>Drum Braking System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>All-Wheel Drive Motorcycles with Disc Braking System utilize discs mounted on the wheels, which are clamped by calipers to slow down the bike. This system is more efficient and offers better stopping power compared to Drum Braking System. On the other hand, Drum Braking System uses a drum that rotates with the wheel, and as the brakes are applied, the brake shoes expand against the drum's inner surface to slow down the bike. While this system is simpler and cost-effective, it is less effective than disc brakes.</p></p>
