<p><p>There are two types of Manual Lensmeters available in the market. Visual Type Lensmeters are designed with an eyepiece where users directly observe the target, making it suitable for small-scale operations. On the other hand, Projection Type Lensmeters display the target image on a screen, enabling multiple users to view it simultaneously. They are commonly used in larger settings such as optical shops or clinics. Both types facilitate the measurement of lens power and other parameters to ensure accurate prescription-based eyewear.</p></p>
