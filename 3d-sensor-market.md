<p><p>The 3D sensor market consists of various types of technologies such as stereo vision, structured light, time of flight, and ultrasound. Stereo vision uses two cameras to create a 3D image by comparing the images from each camera. Structured light projects a pattern onto an object and measures distortions to create a 3D model. Time of flight measures the time it takes for light to bounce back from an object to determine its distance. Ultrasound uses sound waves to create 3D images of objects.</p></p>
