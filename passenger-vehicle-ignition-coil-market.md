<p><ul><li>Single-spark</li><li>Multi-spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passenger Vehicle Ignition Coil market includes two main types: Single-spark and Multi-spark. Single-spark ignition coils produce one spark per ignition cycle, which is commonly used in older vehicles. On the other hand, Multi-spark ignition coils produce multiple sparks per ignition cycle, resulting in a more efficient combustion process and improved engine performance. Both types have their advantages and are used in different vehicles based on their specific requirements and performance needs.</p></p>
