<p><p>Organic Palm Oil market involves two main types - Crude Palm Oil (CPO) and Palm Olein. Crude Palm Oil is obtained directly from the oil palm fruit and is unrefined. It is widely used in various industries for manufacturing purposes. Palm Olein, on the other hand, is a processed form of CPO that undergoes refining to produce a clear liquid fraction. It is popular for its stability at high temperatures and is commonly used for cooking and in the food industry.</p></p>
