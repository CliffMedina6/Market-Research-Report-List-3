<p><p>The medical extension tube market consists of different types of tubes designed for specific purposes. One-way tubes allow the flow of fluid in only one direction, preventing contamination. Two-way tubes enable bidirectional flow, commonly used for drainage or aspiration procedures. Multipath tubes feature multiple channels, allowing the simultaneous administration of various fluids or medications. Each type serves a unique function in medical applications, collectively enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of fluid management and treatment processes.</p></p>
