<p><p>Safety venous blood collection needles come in two market types: soft-connected and hard-connected. Soft-connected needles have a flexible tubing that connects the needle to the collection vessel, allowing for easy movement while the sample is being collected. This provides greater comfort and flexibility for both the patient and the healthcare professional. On the other hand, hard-connected needles have a rigid connection between the needle and the collection vessel, ensuring a secure and stable collection process. Both types aim to provide a safe and efficient blood collection experience for healthcare settings.</p></p>
