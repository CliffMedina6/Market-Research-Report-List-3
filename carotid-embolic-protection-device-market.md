<p><strong>The Carotid Embolic Protection Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Total Artery Coverage</li><li>Non-total Artery Coverage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carotid Embolic Protection Device market is categorized into two types: Total Artery Coverage and Non-total Artery Coverage. Total Artery Coverage devices provide protection for the entire carotid artery during procedures such as stenting, minimizing the risk of embolism. Non-total Artery Coverage devices, on the other hand, only cover a portion of the artery, leaving some areas vulnerable to emboli. Both types of devices aim to reduce the risk of stroke and other complications during carotid artery procedures.</p></p>
