<p><p>FEVE Fluorocarbon Coatings are used in both liquid coating and powder coating markets. In the liquid coating market, FEVE coatings are applied as a liquid onto a surface and then cured to create a durable and protective fluorocarbon finish. In the powder coating market, FEVE coatings are applied as a dry powder onto a surface and then baked to form a tough and long-lasting fluorocarbon coating. Both types of coatings offer excellent weatherability, chemical resistance, and color retention properties.</p></p>
