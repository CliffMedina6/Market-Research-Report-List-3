<p><p>Medical dry imaging films are available in two main types: blue film base and clear film base. Blue film base imaging films are commonly used for general radiography and provide high contrast images with excellent visibility of anatomical details. Clear film base imaging films are used for mammography and provide high resolution images with low noise levels. Both types of films are essential in the healthcare industry for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
