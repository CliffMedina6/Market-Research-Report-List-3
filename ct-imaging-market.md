<p><ul><li>X-ray CT</li><li>Positron Emission Tomography (PET)</li><li>Single-photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CT Imaging market consists of three major types: X-ray CT, Positron Emission Tomography (PET), and Single-photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT). X-ray CT utilizes X-ray technology to produce detailed cross-sectional images of the body, aiding in diagnosing various conditions. PET scans use a small amount of radioactive material to highlight areas of disease or abnormality within the body. SPECT imaging also employs radioactive substances but records the gamma ray emissions, providing information about organ function. These imaging techniques play crucial roles in medical diagnostics and enhancing patient care.</p></p>
