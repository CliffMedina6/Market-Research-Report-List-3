<p><p>Restaurant CRM Market Types refer to the different ways in which CRM software for restaurants is deployed and accessed. On-Premise CRM refers to the traditional model where the software is installed on the restaurant's servers and managed internally. It provides more control but demands higher maintenance costs. On the other hand, Cloud-Based CRM is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It offers easy scalability, lower costs, and automatic updates. Choosing between the two depends on factors such as budget, IT infrastructure, and data security requirements.</p></p>
