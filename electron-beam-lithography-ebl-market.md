<p><strong>The Electron Beam Lithography (EBL) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermionic Sources</li><li>Field Electron Emission Sources</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electron Beam Lithography (EBL) market is divided into two types based on the sources of electrons used for lithography. Thermionic Sources rely on heating a filament to emit electrons, which are then focused into a beam for lithography. On the other hand, Field Electron Emission Sources use a high voltage electric field to extract electrons from a sharp tip or surface, forming the beam for lithography. Both types have their own advantages and limitations, catering to different requirements in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
