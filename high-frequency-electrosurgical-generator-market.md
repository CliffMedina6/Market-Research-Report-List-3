<p><strong>The High Frequency Electrosurgical Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar Circuit</li><li>Bipolar Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Frequency Electrosurgical Generator Market Types include Monopolar Circuit and Bipolar Circuit. Monopolar Circuit uses a single electrode for cutting or coagulating tissue, allowing for deep penetration but requiring a dispersive electrode pad for patient safety. Bipolar Circuit uses two electrodes that are close together, reducing the risk of unintended tissue damage and eliminating the need for a dispersive pad. Each type has its own advantages and is used in various surgical procedures based on the specific requirements of the operation.</p></p>
