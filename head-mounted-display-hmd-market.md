<p><p>Head Mounted Display (HMD) market is categorized into Slide-on HMD, Discrete HMD, and Integrated HMD types. Slide-on HMDs are attached to an existing helmet or eyewear, allowing for easy customization. Discrete HMDs are standalone devices worn on the head, providing immersive visual experiences. Integrated HMDs are built directly into helmets or glasses for a seamless user experience. Each type offers unique advantages and caters to different needs in industries such as healthcare, gaming, military, and automotive.</p></p>
