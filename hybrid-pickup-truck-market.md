<p><p>The hybrid pickup truck market consists of three main types: Plug-in Hybrid Pickup Truck, Mild Hybrid Electrical Pickup Truck, and Full Hybrid Electrical Pickup Truck. Plug-in hybrid trucks can be charged from an external power source, offering extended electric-only driving range. Mild hybrid trucks use a smaller electric motor to assist the gasoline engine in certain situations, improving fuel efficiency. Full hybrid trucks can operate on electric power alone at low speeds and use regenerative braking to recharge the battery while driving.</p></p>
