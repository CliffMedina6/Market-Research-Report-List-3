<p><p>Hydrotreating catalysts market can be classified into two main types based on load type and non-load type. Load type catalysts contain active metals like nickel and molybdenum supported on a carrier material, while non-load type catalysts do not contain any active metals. Load type catalysts are typically more expensive but have higher catalytic activity, while non-load type catalysts are more cost-effective but may require higher operating temperatures. Both types play a crucial role in the hydroprocessing of crude oil and petroleum products.</p></p>
