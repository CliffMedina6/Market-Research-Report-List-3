<p><p>Medical cryogenic storage equipment includes a variety of devices designed to store biological samples and medical supplies at specific temperature ranges. Blood refrigerators maintain temperatures of 4℃ with a variation of ±1℃, while medical refrigerators are kept at 2℃-8℃. Biomedical freezers range from -10℃ to -50℃, ultra-low freezers reach temperatures of -50℃ to -150℃, and liquid nitrogen tanks can go as low as -150℃ to -196℃. Other equipment may include cryogenic storage containers, transport dewars, and thawing systems for various medical applications.</p></p>
