<p><strong>The Metal Thermal Spray Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Combustion Flame Process</li><li>Electrical Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal thermal spray coating market types include combustion flame process and electrical process. Combustion flame process involves spraying molten metal particles onto a surface using a heat source such as a flame or torch. This method is commonly used for applying coatings on large and complex surfaces. Electrical process, on the other hand, involves using an electric arc or plasma to generate the heat needed to melt and spray the metal particles onto the surface. This method is more precise and is often used for smaller, high-precision applications.</p></p>
