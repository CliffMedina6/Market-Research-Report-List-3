<p><p>Silicone room temperature glue is available in two main types: condensed type and addition type. Condensed type silicone room temperature glue is typically a one-part system that cures through the release of acetic acid during the curing process. Addition type silicone room temperature glue, on the other hand, is a two-part system that cures through the addition of a curing agent, which triggers cross-linking. Both types of silicone room temperature glue offer strong adhesive properties and are commonly used in various industries for bonding and sealing applications.</p></p>
