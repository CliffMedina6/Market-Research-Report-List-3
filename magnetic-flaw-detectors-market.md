<p><p>Magnetic Flaw Detectors come in two main types: Magnetic Yokes and Portable Units, and Magnetic Benches. Magnetic Yokes and Portable Units are handheld devices that are used for on-site inspection of ferromagnetic materials. They are convenient for detecting surface defects and discontinuities in hard-to-reach areas. On the other hand, Magnetic Benches are stationary machines that are used for the inspection of larger components in a controlled environment. They provide a more accurate and detailed inspection of materials, making them suitable for industrial applications.</p></p>
