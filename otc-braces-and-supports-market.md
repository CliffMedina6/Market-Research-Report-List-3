<p><p>The OTC Braces and Supports market encompasses two main types: Lower Extremity Braces and Supports, and Upper Extremity Braces and Supports. Lower Extremity Braces and Supports refer to products designed to support and stabilize joints in the legs, such as knees, ankles, and feet. These braces are commonly used for conditions like arthritis, sprains, and ligament injuries. On the other hand, Upper Extremity Braces and Supports are devices that aid in the support and protection of joints in the arms, including wrists, elbows, and shoulders. These braces are commonly used for conditions like tendinitis, carpal tunnel syndrome, and fractures.</p></p>
