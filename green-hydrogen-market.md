<p><p>Green Hydrogen is produced through water electrolysis using renewable energy sources. There are three main types of electrolyzers used in the market: Alkaline (ALK), Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM), and Solid Oxide Electrolyzer. ALK electrolyzers are cost-effective and widely used in large-scale applications. PEM electrolyzers are more efficient and suitable for smaller applications. Solid Oxide Electrolyzers operate at high temperatures and can achieve high efficiency levels. Each type of electrolyzer has its own advantages and is used in different segments of the green hydrogen market.</p></p>
