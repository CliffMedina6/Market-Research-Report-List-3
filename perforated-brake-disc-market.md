<p><ul><li>Ventilated Perforated Brake Disc</li><li>Perforated and Slotted Brake Disc</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Perforated Brake Disc market includes two main types: ventilated perforated brake discs and perforated and slotted brake discs. Ventilated perforated brake discs have holes throughout the disc to improve cooling and reduce brake fade. On the other hand, perforated and slotted brake discs have both holes and slots, which are designed to dissipate heat even more effectively and provide better braking performance under high stress conditions. Both types of brake discs are popular choices among performance car enthusiasts and racing vehicles.</p></p>
