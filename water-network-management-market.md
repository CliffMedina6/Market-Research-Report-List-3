<p><p>Water network management involves managing and monitoring water distribution networks to ensure the efficient and sustainable supply of water. The increasing demand for efficient water distribution systems, coupled with the need to reduce non-revenue water losses, is driving the market growth. Rapid urbanization, population growth, and the aging infrastructure are further contributing to the adoption of water network management solutions.</p><p>Furthermore, stringent government regulations for water conservation and the increasing focus on sustainability are also fueling the market growth.
