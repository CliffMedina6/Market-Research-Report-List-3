<p><p>The emergency and rescue stretcher market consists of two main types: hydraulic stretchers and manual stretchers. Hydraulic stretchers are equipped with hydraulic systems that allow for easy adjustment of height and angles, providing convenience for medical professionals during patient transfer. Manual stretchers, on the other hand, require physical exertion from the users for adjustment. While hydraulic stretchers offer enhanced efficiency and ease of use, manual stretchers are often more cost-effective. Both types serve the purpose of safely transporting patients during emergencies and rescue operations.</p></p>
