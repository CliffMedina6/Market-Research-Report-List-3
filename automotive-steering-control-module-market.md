<p><strong>The Automotive Steering Control Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Power-Assisted Steering (EPAS)</li><li>Electric Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Steering Control Module market is categorized into two main types: Power-Assisted Steering (EPAS) and Electric Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS). </p><p>EPAS utilizes an electric motor to provide assistance to the driver, resulting in easier and more precise steering control. EHPS, on the other hand, combines the benefits of electric and hydraulic power steering systems, utilizing an electric motor to drive a hydraulic pump.</p><p>Both systems offer enhanced steering performance and efficiency, improving overall handling and driver comfort in vehicles.</p></p>
