<p><ul><li>Drylaid Nonwovens</li><li>Wetlaid Nonwovens</li><li>Spunbond Nonwovens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sustainable nonwoven markets consist of various types such as drylaid nonwovens, wetlaid nonwovens, and spunbond nonwovens. Drylaid nonwovens are produced by laying fibers on a conveyor belt and bonding them together through mechanical or thermal processes. Wetlaid nonwovens involve fibers being dispersed in water and then bonded together. Spunbond nonwovens are made by extruding molten polymer through a spinneret and then bonding the resulting fibers. Each type offers unique properties and benefits for sustainable applications in various industries.</p></p>
