<p><p>Flight tracking system market includes various types such as ADS-B, FANS, and PFTS. ADS-B (Automatic Dependent Surveillance-Broadcast) uses satellite signals to track aircraft in real-time, providing accurate position data. FANS (Future Air Navigation System) incorporates data link technology to improve communication and surveillance between aircraft and air traffic control. PFTS (Flight Tracking System) offers a comprehensive solution for monitoring flight operations, including tracking, analysis, and reporting. These systems play a crucial role in enhancing the safety and efficiency of air travel.</p></p>
