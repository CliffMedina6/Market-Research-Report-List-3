<p><p>The mobile orthopedic phototherapy lamp market consists of two main types: infrared and visible light. Infrared lamps emit light rays that are not visible to the human eye but can penetrate deep into the skin, providing therapeutic benefits for conditions like muscle and joint pain. On the other hand, visible light lamps emit light that is visible to the human eye and are commonly used for skin conditions and wound healing. Both types of lamps are portable and offer convenient options for individuals seeking orthopedic phototherapy treatments on-the-go.</p></p>
