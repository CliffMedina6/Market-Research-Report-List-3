<p><p>Treatment Planning Systems (TPS) market can be categorized into two types: Cloud-based and On-premise. Cloud-based TPS involves the use of web-based software that is hosted on remote servers, providing scalability, accessibility, and cost-effective solutions for healthcare facilities. On the other hand, On-premise TPS involves the installation and maintenance of software on local servers, providing more control and customization options but requiring higher upfront costs and limited accessibility. These types offer different advantages and disadvantages, allowing healthcare providers to choose the most suitable option based on their specific needs and resources.</p></p>
