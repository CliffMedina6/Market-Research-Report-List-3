<p><p>The digital construction management market comprises two types of solutions - cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on external servers, accessible through the internet. They offer flexible access, scalability, and convenience, requiring limited IT resources. On the other hand, on-premise solutions are installed and operated within the organization's infrastructure. They provide greater control over data, but require higher upfront costs and maintenance. Ultimately, the choice between these types depends on factors like budget, security needs, and organizational preferences.</p></p>
