<p><p>The Subway Sliding Door System market can be categorized into two main types: Spindle Unit and Toothbelt Unit. Spindle Unit systems utilize a spindle mechanism to open and close the doors, offering a reliable and economical solution for subway entrances. On the other hand, Toothbelt Unit systems employ a toothed belt to operate the doors, providing a more efficient and quieter option for subway access points. Both market types cater to the varying needs and preferences of subway operators and commuters.</p></p>
