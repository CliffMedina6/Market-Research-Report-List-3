<p><p>Virtual infrastructure manager market types can be categorized into two main categories: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises virtual infrastructure managers are software solutions that are installed and deployed on servers within an organization's own physical infrastructure. They require dedicated hardware, maintenance, and management. In contrast, cloud-based virtual infrastructure managers are accessed over the internet and do not require any hardware or software installation. They are provided as a service by cloud service providers, allowing organizations to easily scale and manage their virtual infrastructure without the need for on-site hardware or maintenance.</p></p>
