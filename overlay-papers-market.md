<p><p>Overlay papers are classified into three different market types based on their weight: below 27 g/m², 27 g/m²-45 g/m², and above 45 g/m². Below 27 g/m² overlay papers are lightweight and translucent, making them ideal for use in applications where thin, delicate paper is needed. Papers in the 27 g/m²-45 g/m² range offer a balance between weight and durability, suitable for a wide range of overlay applications. Overlay papers above 45 g/m² are heavier and more robust, often used for applications requiring a sturdier material.</p></p>
