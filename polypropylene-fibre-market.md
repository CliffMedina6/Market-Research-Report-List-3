<p><p>Polypropylene fibre market can be categorized into two main types: Polypropylene Staple Fibre and Continuous Fibre. Polypropylene Staple Fibre is short in length and is commonly used in applications such as textiles, carpets, and non-woven fabrics. On the other hand, Continuous Fibre is long in length and is often used in applications that require high strength and durability, such as automotive components, geotextiles, and industrial filters. These two types cater to different market demands and offer specific advantages based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>
