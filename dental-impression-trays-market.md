<p><p>Dental impression trays are essential tools used in dentistry for making accurate and detailed molds of a patient's teeth and gums. These trays are typically made of plastic or metal and come in various sizes and shapes to accommodate different dental arch sizes and types.</p><p>The dental impression trays market is projected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include the increasing prevalence of dental disorders, rising geriatric population, and growing awareness regarding oral healthcare. Additionally, technological advancements in impression materials and techniques, such as the development of digital impression systems, are also contributing to market growth.</p><p>The market for dental impression trays is witnessing several trends. One such trend is the adoption of disposable impression trays. These trays offer advantages such as reduced risk of cross-contamination, ease of use, and cost-effectiveness. Furthermore, there is a gradual shift towards the use of metal impression trays due to their superior strength and durability compared to plastic trays. Metal trays also allow for better heat transfer during the setting of impression materials.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the integration of digital dentistry and CAD/CAM technology. Digital impression systems eliminate the need for traditional impression trays, providing a more comfortable experience for patients and better accuracy in capturing dental details. This trend is expected to drive the demand for intraoral scanning devices and decrease the dependence on conventional trays.</p><p>In conclusion, the dental impression trays market is projected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
