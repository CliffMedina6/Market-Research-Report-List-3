<p><p>The SUV suction door market comprises two main types: OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and Aftermarket. These doors are specifically designed and engineered to meet the specifications and quality standards of the vehicle brand. Aftermarket, on the other hand, refers to doors that are produced by third-party manufacturers and sold separately from the vehicle manufacturer.
