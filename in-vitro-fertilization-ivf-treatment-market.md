<p><p>In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) treatment encompasses several types of assisted reproductive technologies, including VF-ET, ICSI, and PGD. VF-ET involves fertilizing an egg with sperm in a laboratory setting before transferring the resulting embryo into the uterus. ICSI involves injecting a single sperm directly into an egg to facilitate fertilization. PGD, or preimplantation genetic diagnosis, is a technique used to screen embryos for genetic disorders before implantation. These different types of IVF treatments offer varying methods to help individuals or couples achieve successful pregnancy.</p></p>
