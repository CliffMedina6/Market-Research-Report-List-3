<p><p>Stone retrieval devices are used in minimally invasive procedures to remove kidney or ureteral stones. Nitinol stone baskets are made of a superelastic, nickel-titanium alloy which allows for flexibility and durability during stone retrieval. Stainless steel stone baskets, on the other hand, are made of a rigid and sturdy material. Both types of stone baskets are widely used in urological procedures, with Nitinol baskets favored for their flexibility and ability to navigate through narrow passages, while stainless steel baskets are chosen for their strength and durability.</p></p>
