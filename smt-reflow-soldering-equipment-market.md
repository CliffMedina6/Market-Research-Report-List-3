<p><p>SMT reflow soldering equipment market includes different types of equipment such as gas phase welding equipment and infrared welding equipment. Gas phase welding equipment uses gases to create a controlled atmosphere for soldering, ensuring high-quality joints. Infrared welding equipment, on the other hand, uses infrared radiation to heat up the components and melt the solder, without the need for direct contact with the heating element. Both types of equipment have their own advantages and are widely used in the electronics manufacturing industry.</p></p>
