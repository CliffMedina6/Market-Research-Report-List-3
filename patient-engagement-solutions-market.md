<p><p>Patient engagement solutions refer to tools and technologies that help in engaging patients in their own healthcare. Additionally, the proliferation of smartphones and wearable devices, along with the availability of healthcare apps and remote monitoring devices, is further fueling market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the COVID-19 pandemic has acted as a catalyst for the adoption of patient engagement solutions, as healthcare providers seek to minimize in-person visits and prioritize remote care.
