<p><p>Bamboo based flooring market offers several types of products to cater to different customer needs. Solid bamboo floor is made from solid bamboo stalks and is known for its durability. Engineered bamboo floor consists of a base layer of plywood with a layer of bamboo on top, providing stability and resistance to moisture. Strand woven bamboo floor is made by compressing bamboo fibers together, resulting in a highly durable and scratch-resistant product. Other market includes unique options like cork-infused bamboo flooring or hand-scraped bamboo flooring.</p></p>
