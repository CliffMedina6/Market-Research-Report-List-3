<p><p>Boron steel is categorized into two main market types based on its boron content: Rigid boron steel contains a boron content of 0.001%-0.005%, making it strong and resistant to deformation. On the other hand, Brittle boron steel has a boron content above 0.007%, resulting in a material that is less ductile and more prone to cracking under stress. Both types have their own unique properties and applications in the automotive, construction, and manufacturing industries.</p></p>
