<p><strong>The Liquid Applied Roofing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bituminous Membranes</li><li>Elastomeric Membranes</li><li>Cementitious Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid applied roofing systems consist of three main types: bituminous membranes, elastomeric membranes, and cementitious membranes. Bituminous membranes are made from asphalt or coal-tar pitch and are commonly used in low-slope roofing applications. Elastomeric membranes are made from synthetic rubber or plastic polymers, providing flexibility and durability. Cementitious membranes are composed of Portland cement and fibers, offering excellent waterproofing properties. Each type of membrane offers unique benefits and is suited for different roofing applications based on factors such as climate and building design.</p></p>
