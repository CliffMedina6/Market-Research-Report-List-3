<p><p>Rubber Bearings market can be categorized into different types based on their material and properties. Natural Rubber Bearings use rubber derived directly from rubber trees and provide flexibility and durability. Lead Rubber Bearings contain lead plugs for energy dissipation during seismic movements. High Damping Rubber Bearings offer higher energy dissipation capacity due to their design and material. Other types of Rubber Bearings may include laminated rubber bearings or sliding bearings that cater to specific structural needs. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of vibration isolation and seismic protection.</p></p>
