Maintenance refers to routine inspections and servicing to ensure engines are in good working condition. Repair involves fixing any specific issues or damages identified during maintenance. Overhaul, on the other hand, entails a complete disassembly, examination, repair, and testing of an engine to restore it to a like-new condition. These market types represent the various stages of engine maintenance and are essential for ensuring the safety and reliability of aircraft engines.</p></p>
