<p><p>Data Center Optical Distribution Frames (ODFs) come in three main types: Wall-Mounted ODFs, Floor-Standing ODFs, and Rack-Mounted ODFs. Wall-Mounted ODFs are compact and space-saving, ideal for small data centers or telecom rooms. Floor-Standing ODFs are larger and provide more capacity, suitable for medium to large data centers. Rack-Mounted ODFs are designed to be mounted on equipment racks for easy integration into existing infrastructure. Each type offers unique benefits depending on the size and requirements of the data center.</p></p>
