<p><p>Medical protective clothing refers to attire worn by healthcare professionals to minimize the risk of infection and ensure their safety during medical procedures. This clothing can be categorized into three types: surgical clothing, daily work clothing, and special protective clothing. Surgical clothing is specifically designed for surgical procedures, providing a sterile environment. Daily work clothing is worn by healthcare staff during routine activities and provides basic protection against liquid splashes and solid particles. Special protective clothing is used for hazardous situations such as handling chemicals or dealing with infectious diseases and offers higher levels of protection.</p></p>
