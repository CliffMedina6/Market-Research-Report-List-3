<p><p>The PVA Embolization Particles market is categorized into three types: Conventional/Irregular PVA, Compressible PVA, and Spherical PVA. Conventional/Irregular PVA particles have irregular shapes and sizes, allowing for a variety of occlusion sizes. Compressible PVA particles are designed to be easily compressed and molded, making them suitable for occlusions in curved blood vessels. Spherical PVA particles have a uniform shape and size, providing consistent occlusion efficacy. These different types cater to various treatment needs and preferences, ensuring optimal outcomes for patients requiring embolization procedures.</p></p>
