<p><ul><li>DUV Lithography Systems</li><li>I-Line Lithography Systems</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV photolithography systems are widely used in the semiconductor industry for manufacturing integrated circuits. There are different types of UV photolithography systems available in the market. Deep UV (DUV) lithography systems utilize shorter wavelength UV light for high-resolution patterning. I-Line lithography systems use longer wavelength UV light and are preferred for larger feature sizes. The "Others" category includes alternative UV lithography systems like electron beam lithography and extreme ultraviolet lithography. These systems offer different capabilities and are used depending on the specific requirements of the semiconductor fabrication process.</p></p>
