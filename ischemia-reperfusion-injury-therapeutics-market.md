<p><p>Ischemia reperfusion injury therapeutics refer to the treatment or medications used to mitigate the damage caused by the interruption and subsequent restoration of blood flow to an organ or tissue. This injury occurs when blood supply to an organ or tissue is temporarily blocked, leading to oxygen and nutrient deprivation. The sudden reintroduction of blood flow causes inflammation and oxidative stress, resulting in tissue damage. Ischemia reperfusion injury can occur in various medical conditions, such as heart attacks, stroke, organ transplantation, and surgeries.</p><p>The Ischemia Reperfusion Injury Therapeutics Market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR (Compound Annual Growth Rate) of 7.6% during the forecast period.
