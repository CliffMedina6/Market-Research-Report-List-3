<p><p>Dose calibrator market types include RSO Nuclides and PET Nuclides. RSO Nuclides refer to radioactive sources that are used for Radiation Safety Officer (RSO) training and calibration purposes. These nuclides are typically used in the healthcare industry to ensure accurate radiation measurements. On the other hand, PET Nuclides pertain to radioactive isotopes that are used in Positron Emission Tomography (PET) scans. These nuclides are essential for diagnosing and monitoring various medical conditions, especially cancer. Both RSO and PET Nuclides play crucial roles in providing accurate radiation measurements and improving healthcare outcomes.</p></p>
