<p><p>Prefilled softgel capsules are available in two main types: gelatin type and non-animal type. Gelatin type softgel capsules are made from animal sources, typically bovine or porcine gelatin. Non-animal type softgel capsules, on the other hand, are made from plant-based materials such as seaweed or starch. Both types are used to enclose liquid or semi-solid substances for oral administration. The choice between gelatin and non-animal type depends on factors such as dietary restrictions, cultural considerations, and market demand for vegetarian or vegan products.</p></p>
