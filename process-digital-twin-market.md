<p><p>The Process Digital Twin market can be categorized into two types: Cloud and On-premises markets. In the Cloud market, digital twins are hosted and operated on remote servers, allowing users to access and utilize the digital twin capabilities through internet connectivity. On the other hand, the On-premises market involves hosting and operating digital twins locally within the organization's infrastructure, providing direct control and management over the digital twin. The choice between the two types depends on factors like data privacy concerns, network connectivity, and customization requirements.</p></p>
