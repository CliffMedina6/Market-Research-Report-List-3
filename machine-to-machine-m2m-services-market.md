Wi-Fi enables wireless communication within short distances, Ethernet uses wired connections, Zigbee is a low-power wireless technology, Satellite connects devices using orbiting satellites, and Power-Line utilizes existing power lines to transmit data. These different market types provide diverse options for M2M communication, allowing devices to seamlessly connect and exchange information in various environments.</p></p>
