<p><p>Injection Grade Chlorinated Polyvinylchloride (CPVC) Resin is available in two main types - High Chlorine and Low Chloride. High Chlorine CPVC resins are produced with a higher chlorine content, making them more heat and chemical resistant, ideal for applications requiring superior performance under harsh conditions. Low Chloride CPVC resins have lower chlorine content, offering improved flow properties for easier processing during injection molding. Both types cater to different market needs, with High Chlorine CPVCs focusing on durability and Low Chloride CPVCs emphasizing ease of processing.</p></p>
