<p><p>Seaweed biofertilizers come in two main types - powder and liquid. Powder types are typically dried and ground seaweed that can be easily mixed into the soil for plant uptake. Liquid types are extracted from seaweed and diluted in water for application as a foliar spray or through irrigation systems. Both types of seaweed biofertilizers provide essential nutrients and growth-promoting hormones to plants, leading to increased yield, improved crop quality, and enhanced soil health. Each type offers unique benefits and application methods for farmers and gardeners.</p></p>
