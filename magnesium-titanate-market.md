It is a combination of magnesium oxide (MgO) and titanium dioxide (TiO2) and is commonly known as magnesium metatitanate. The rising demand for electronic devices and the miniaturization trend in the electronics industry are driving the growth of the magnesium titanate market.</p><p>Additionally, the automotive industry is another significant driver for the market growth.
