<p><p>Automatic Expansion Valves (AEVs) are used in refrigeration and air conditioning systems to regulate the flow of refrigerant. There are two main types of AEVs in the market: those without bypass and those with bypass. AEVs without bypass have a fixed opening size and are suitable for applications where there is a consistent load. On the other hand, AEVs with bypass have an adjustable opening size, making them ideal for systems with varying loads. Both types of AEVs help maintain proper refrigerant flow, improving system efficiency and performance.</p></p>
