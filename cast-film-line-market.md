<p><p>The cast film line market includes three main types: CPP cast film line, CPE cast film line, and others. CPP (cast polypropylene) cast film lines are commonly used for food packaging due to their high clarity and excellent moisture barrier properties. CPE (cast polyethylene) cast film lines are often used for medical packaging and industrial applications due to their superior puncture resistance and flexibility. The "others" category encompasses various types of cast film lines designed for specific applications such as agriculture, construction, and automotive industries.</p></p>
