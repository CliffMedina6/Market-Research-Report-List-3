<p><p>Orthopaedic devices market types include drill guides, guide tubes, implant holders, custom clamps, screw drivers, and others. Drill guides are used to ensure accurate placement of screws during orthopaedic surgeries. Guide tubes assist in guiding wires or pins through bones. Implant holders securely hold orthopaedic implants in place. Custom clamps are designed to secure fractured bone fragments. Screw drivers aid in inserting and removing screws during procedures. Lastly, "others" encompass various devices used in orthopaedic surgeries, such as bone reamers, plates, and saws, among others. These devices play crucial roles in facilitating surgical precision and successful orthopaedic procedures.</p></p>
