<p><p>PVC resins can be classified into two main types based on their production processes - Ethylene Process and Calcium Carbide Process. The Ethylene Process involves the use of ethylene gas to produce PVC resin, while the Calcium Carbide Process involves the use of calcium carbide to produce the resin. Both processes have their own advantages and disadvantages in terms of cost, efficiency, and environmental impact, leading to different market dynamics for each type of PVC resin.</p></p>
