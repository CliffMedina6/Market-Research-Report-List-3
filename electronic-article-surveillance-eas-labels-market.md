<p><p>Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) Labels are security tags that are used to protect merchandise from theft in retail stores. These small, adhesive labels are typically attached to products and can be easily detected by electronic sensors located at the store's exit points. When a tagged item passes through these sensors, an alarm is triggered, alerting store personnel of potential theft.</p><p>The EAS Labels market has been witnessing significant growth in recent years due to the rising instances of retail theft and shoplifting, which have driven the demand for improved security solutions. The market is also driven by the increasing adoption of retail analytics and real-time inventory management systems by retailers.</p><p>Moreover, the advent of advanced technologies, such as RFID (Radio-Frequency Identification) and RFID-enabled EAS labels, has further boosted the market growth. RFID-enabled EAS labels offer enhanced security, accuracy, and efficiency in tracking and managing inventory, making them increasingly preferred by retailers.</p><p>Additionally, the growing popularity of organized retail sectors and the rapid expansion of retail chains across the globe have further fueled the demand for EAS labels.
