<p><ul><li>Deformed Steel Bar</li><li>Mild Steel Steel Bar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel bar market consists of two main types: deformed steel bars and mild steel bars. Deformed steel bars are designed with a ribbed surface to provide better bonding with concrete, making them suitable for construction projects that require high tensile strength. Mild steel bars, on the other hand, are more flexible and have a smooth surface, making them ideal for smaller construction projects like residential buildings. Both types of steel bars are essential in the construction industry for reinforcing concrete structures and providing structural support.</p></p>
