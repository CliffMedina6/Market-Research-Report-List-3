<p><p>Automotive Parts Aluminium Die Casting Market Types include Pressure Die Casting, Vacuum Die Casting, Squeeze Die Casting, and Semi-Solid Die Casting. Pressure Die Casting involves injecting molten metal into a mold under high pressure. Vacuum Die Casting uses a vacuum to remove air from the mold cavity before pouring molten metal. Squeeze Die Casting involves applying pressure to the molten metal to force it into the mold. Semi-Solid Die Casting uses partially solidified metal to reduce shrinkage and improve mechanical properties.</p></p>
