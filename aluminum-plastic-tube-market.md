<p><p>The aluminum plastic tube market is categorized into two types: Type I and Type II. Type II, on the other hand, consists of tubes where the aluminum body is joined with a plastic layer, offering enhanced flexibility and impact resistance. Such tubes are commonly used in the packaging of food products and household chemicals.</p></p>
