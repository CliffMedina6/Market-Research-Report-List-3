<p><p>Wood-based activated carbon is available in three main types in the market: columnar, granular, and powder. Columnar activated carbon is typically used for gas purification and has a high mechanical strength. Granular activated carbon is commonly used in water treatment due to its larger surface area for adsorption. Powder activated carbon is finely ground and has quick adsorption rates, making it suitable for liquid phase applications. Each type offers unique properties and benefits for different industrial and environmental uses.</p></p>
