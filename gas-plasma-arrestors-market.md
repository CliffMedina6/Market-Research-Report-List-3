<p><p>Gas Plasma Arrestors are commonly categorized into Type I and Type II markets. Type I arrestors are designed for locations where lightning activity is frequent and direct strikes are a common occurrence. They are engineered to handle high surge currents and provide superior protection for sensitive equipment. On the other hand, Type II arrestors are suitable for areas with moderate lightning activity and offer protection against indirect lightning strikes. Both Type I and Type II markets cater to specific needs based on the level of lightning exposure in different environments.</p></p>
