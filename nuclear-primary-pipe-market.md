<p><p>The Nuclear Primary Pipe Market is segmented into various types based on different reactor technologies. Pressurized Water Reactors (PWR) use pressurized water to transfer heat and generate electricity. Boiling Water Reactors (BWR) involve boiling water to produce steam for power generation. Pressurized Heavy Water Reactors (PHWR) use heavy water as both coolant and moderator. Gas Cooled Reactors (GCR) use carbon dioxide as a coolant. Light Water Graphite Reactors (LWGR) use graphite as a moderator. Fast Breeder Reactors (FBR) breed more fissile material than they consume.</p></p>
