<p><p>Solar PV generators come in two main types - Monocrystalline Silicon and Polycrystalline Silicon. Monocrystalline Silicon panels are made from a single continuous crystal structure, making them more efficient in converting sunlight into electricity. On the other hand, Polycrystalline Silicon panels are made from multiple silicon fragments, making them less expensive but slightly less efficient. Both types have their advantages and are commonly used in residential, commercial, and utility-scale solar installations.</p></p>
