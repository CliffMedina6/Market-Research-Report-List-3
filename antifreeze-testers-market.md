<p><strong>The Antifreeze Testers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Float</li><li>Disc</li><li>Needle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Antifreeze testers come in three main types: float, disc, and needle. Float testers work by floating in the coolant and indicating the freezing point. Disc testers have a disc that changes color based on the antifreeze concentration. Needle testers use a needle that points to the freezing point on a scale. Each type offers a different method of measuring the freezing point of antifreeze, providing users with an easy way to ensure their vehicle's cooling system is protected in cold weather.</p></p>
