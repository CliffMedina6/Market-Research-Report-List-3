<p><p>Automotive circuit protection components are essential in ensuring the safety and reliability of electrical systems in vehicles. </p><p>Automotive Blade Fuses are used to protect circuits from overcurrent situations by breaking the circuit when a specified current is exceeded. Automotive Grade PTC Thermistors provide overcurrent protection by increasing resistance as temperature rises due to excessive current. Automotive Grade TVS Diodes protect against voltage spikes and surges, safeguarding sensitive electronic components. Polymeric ESD suppressors prevent damage from electrostatic discharge. Other components in the market include circuit breakers, resettable fuses, and varistors.</p></p>
