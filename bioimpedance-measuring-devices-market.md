<p><strong>The Bioimpedance Measuring Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-frequency Bioimpedance Devices</li><li>Multiple-frequency Bioimpedance Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bioimpedance measuring devices are categorized into single-frequency and multiple-frequency devices. Single-frequency devices measure the impedance of the body at one specific frequency, typically 50 kHz, and are commonly used for basic body composition analysis. On the other hand, multiple-frequency devices measure impedance at various frequencies, providing more detailed and accurate information about body composition, such as intracellular and extracellular water levels. Multiple-frequency devices are more advanced and can offer a more comprehensive assessment of an individual's health.</p></p>
