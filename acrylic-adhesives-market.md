<p><strong>The Acrylic Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Reactive</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acrylic adhesives are classified into four main types based on their composition and curing mechanisms: water-based, solvent-based, reactive, and other. Water-based acrylic adhesives are environmentally friendly and have low VOC emissions. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding properties but emit harmful fumes during curing. Reactive adhesives require a chemical reaction to cure and offer high bond strength. The "other" category includes specialty acrylic adhesives with unique properties suited for specific applications, such as UV-curable adhesives or pressure-sensitive adhesives.</p></p>
