<p><p>The Utility Locating System market includes various types such as Electromagnetic Field, Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR), and others. Electromagnetic Field technology uses electromagnetic signals to detect underground utilities. Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR) technology uses radar pulses to image the subsurface and identify buried utilities. Other types of utility locating systems may include acoustic systems or infrared technology. These systems are essential for construction and excavation projects to prevent damage to underground utilities and ensure worker safety.</p></p>
