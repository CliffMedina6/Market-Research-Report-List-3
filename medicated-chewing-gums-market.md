<p><p>Medicated chewing gums are available in two main types: solid and semi-solid. Solid medicated chewing gums are firm and have a hard texture. They typically require more effort to chew and release the medication slowly. On the other hand, semi-solid medicated chewing gums are softer and easier to chew. Both solid and semi-solid forms offer advantages depending on the specific medication and desired therapeutic effects.</p></p>
